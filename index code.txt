          <ul>
            <li><strong>Instant communication:</strong> Calls, texts and video keep us connected.</li>
            <li><strong>Knowledge at hand:</strong> Access to online courses, tutorials and e-books.</li>
            <li><strong>Multi-functionality:</strong> One device for many needs — navigation, banking, photography.</li>
            <li><strong>Emergency lifeline:</strong> Quick contact during urgent situations.</li>

        <section id="disadvantages" class="card" style="margin-top:14px">
          <h2>The Dark Side — Disadvantages</h2>
          <ul>
            <li><strong>Addiction risk:</strong> Excessive gaming and social media reduce focus.</li>
            <li><strong>Sleep disruption:</strong> Late-night usage harms sleep and concentration.</li>
            <li><strong>Distraction hazard:</strong> Using phones while driving or in class causes accidents and reduces productivity.</li>
            <li><strong>Health concerns:</strong> Eye strain, lowered physical activity, and concerns about radiation exposure.</li>
          </ul>

        <section id="tips" class="card" style="margin-top:14px">
          <h2>Practical Tips to Reduce Overuse</h2>
          <ul class="tips">
            <li><strong>Set phone-free times:</strong> Especially before bedtime to improve sleep.</li>
            <li><strong>Use screen-time apps:</strong> Track and limit daily usage.</li>
            <li><strong>Disable non-essential notifications:</strong> Reduce interruptions.</li>
            <li><strong>Prioritise real-world interactions:</strong> Make time for face-to-face conversations.</li>
          </ul>

          <h3>Healthy Habits for Students</h3>
          <ul>
            <li>Use phones primarily for study and communication; limit gaming/social media.</li>
            <li>Charge phones outside the bedroom overnight.</li>
            <li>Schedule study breaks to rest eyes and move physically.</li>
            <li>Keep open communication with parents about responsible usage.</li>
          </ul>

          <h3>Success Story</h3>
          <p>A student restricted gaming to 30 minutes daily and saw improvements in sleep and grades after parents and teachers supported mindful habits.</p>
        </section>

            <li>Use technology to empower yourself — don’t let it control you.</li>
